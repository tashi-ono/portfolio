About Me

   My name is Natasha and I am a full-stack software developer. Nothing is more inspiring and motivating to me than to have the ability to create tools that help people. In transitioning my career to a developer, my goal is to learn how I can use my skills to better serve my community. Whether I am building apps to help people find resources or educate kids on budgeting skills, I want to find a role that allows me to create these service-oriented applications to bring about positive social impact. Beyond career aspirations, I enjoy homebrewing, playing uke & singing, and cooking up some mean, Asian-fusion dishes inspired by my mom.
