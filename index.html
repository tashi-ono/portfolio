About Me

   My name is Natasha and I am a software developer based out of Santa Rosa, CA. Nothing is more inspiring and motivating to me than to have the ability to create tools that help people. In transitioning my career to a developer, my goal is to learn how I can use my skills to better serve my community. Whether I am building apps to improve marketability of local businesses or helping people locate resources, I want to find a role that allows me to create these service-oriented applications to bring about positive social impact.

   Beyond career aspirations, I enjoy homebrewing, playing uke & singing, and cooking up some mean, Asian-fusion dishes inspired by my mom.
